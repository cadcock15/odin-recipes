Cucumber Lime Water

Really gets the guts going

A refreshing fusion of lime and cucumber.

Ingredients

  • 4 cucumbers
  • 1 lime
  • 8oz water

Recipe Instructions

  1. cut up cucumbers
  2. cut and squeeze lime juice into glass
  3. put cucumbers chunks in glass
  4. add 8oz water to glass
  5. let sit overnight
  6. Enjoy!
