Rootbeer float

A Tasty Treat

A yummy blend of cold creamy soda goodness

Ingredients

  • 1 12oz rootbeer can
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • whip cream from aerosol can
  • 1 cherry

Recipe Instructions

  1. pour rootbeer in a chilled glass
  2. scoop icecream on top
  3. top with whipped cream
  4. top with 1 cherry
